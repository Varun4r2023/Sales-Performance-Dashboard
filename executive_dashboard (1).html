💡 Insight: Accessories generate 58.6% margins vs Bikes at 33.2%. Prioritising Accessories in marketing can improve profitability without increasing order volumes.
Key Findings

The United States leads all regions with 32.8% of total revenue ($28.0M), followed by Australia at 25.0%. YoY revenue growth peaked in 2015 at +41.5%, then declined 11.5% in 2016 — warranting investigation into market saturation. Accessories deliver 58.6% profit margins, nearly double Bikes (33.2%), representing a high-value upsell opportunity. The Adults 35–64 segment drives ~50% of all revenue; Seniors (64+) remain underpenetrated at under 0.4%. Caps (14.2%) and Jerseys (14.7%) are the lowest-margin sub-categories and warrant pricing review.
